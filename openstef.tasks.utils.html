openstef.tasks.utils.dependencies.find_groups(pjs: Sequence[openstef.data_classes.prediction_job.PredictionJobDataClass], randomize_groups: bool = False) → Tuple[networkx.classes.digraph.DiGraph, List[List[openstef.data_classes.prediction_job.PredictionJobDataClass]]]¶

Find a sequence of prediction job groups respecting dependencies.

Compute groups of prediction jobs such that the prediction jobs in a group depend of at least one prediction job in the previous group and does not depend on a prediction job in the following groups. This means that all the prediction jobs in a group can be run in parallel and that if groups are treated in the given order, the dependencies of a prediction job have already been treated when the prediction job is run.

Parameters

  • pjs (Iterable[PredictionJobDataClass]) – The sequence of prediction jobs

  • randomize_groups (bool, optional) – whether subgroups should be randomized.

Returns

the dependency graph List[List[PredictionJobDataClass]]: The list of prediction job groups

Return type
